Walnut Cornbread

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup of cooked corn
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups of chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
  2. Put ingredients into a food processor and puree until smooth.
  3. Put in pan and bake in oven for 45 minutes or until it is brown on top.
  4. Enjoy!
